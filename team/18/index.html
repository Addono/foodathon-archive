About us

We are four young researchers from three different countries namely Togo; Ghana and Gambia with expertise in crop genetics and agronomic diversity, biochemistry, tissue culture and varieties selection as well as phytochemical and anti-nutritional detection in plants and quality control. Our vision of the team is to contribute to eradicating hunger.

Solution

Our solution is creating an App named FARM HOUSE ALERT which will propagate genetic diversity information on crop varieties resulting to prevent crop from going extinct by involving the citizen scientist and the farmer to arrive at zero hunger.
