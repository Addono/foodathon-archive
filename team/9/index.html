Solution

We developed an innovative solution to increase the profitability of rural farmers and create jobs for youths. Agri-Pro is an APP that helps to link rural farmers to processors for value creation. The APP locates small holder processors in a specific location then connect to the farmers for food processing. The App helps to eliminate middlemen and can help to reduce upto 40% post harvest waste through prompt linkage to the nearest processor. In general, our App is based on contract manufacturing using an inclusive business model. Please click the video to see our innovation. Thanks!
